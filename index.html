Nyah Mattison

First-year Masters student at the UC Berkeley School of Information, specializing in UX Research and Product Management.

As a designer and as a developer, I am dedicated to prioritizing human-centered and empathic design that foregrounds collaboration, creativity, and compassion above all else. My specific research interests lie in the inculcation of race and gender bias within digital spaces, and how we can use human-centered design to mitigate it.

Prior to my studies at Cal, I received a BA in Computer Science and Digital Arts and Media from Transylvania University in Lexington, Kentucky. During my undergraduate experience, I performed research on the work of writing centers to reduce community anxiety in times of crisis and on the phenomenon of digital minstrelsy in online spaces.
